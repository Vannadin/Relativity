Concepts

A · γ (mono)

The Lorentz-factor glyph — the signature of relativity. Cleanest, most legible at 38px. Top pick for the toolbar.

A2 · γ + light streak

Same γ with a faint diagonal light-streak for a hint of speed. Slightly livelier, still clean.

B · light wall (asymptote)

A curve rising toward a c wall it can never cross — the mechanic itself, abstracted. Elegant; reads as "the barrier".

C · doppler chevrons

Aft-red → fore-blue shift on forward chevrons. Colorful, pops on a SpaceDock thumbnail; busier at 38px.

D · two clocks (twin paradox)

One dial, two diverging hands — mission time vs crew time. Conceptually rich; a touch detailed for tiny sizes.

E · γ on doppler disc (combo)

γ over a blue→red disc. Identity + color in one — strongest as the large SpaceDock/logo thumbnail.
